Dear Data (2016) by Georgia Lupi & Stefanie Posavec

Data Portrait

Objective

Create a data portrait out of a personal habit of yours.

Description

For Assignment 6, you will spend a week tracking a personal habit of yours to create a data-self portrait. Choose the personal habit mindfully — you will need to collect 20+ data points for this assignment. Record the data onto a numbered list every time it happens, and translate the list into array(s) in p5.js. Visualize your data intentionally so that it tells a meaningful story of your habit.

Design Constraint

Canvas Size: 600 x 600

Timeline

Due Date: 10/19

  1. Decide what kind of data you’d like to track, and collect seven days worth of data (20+ data points) in the form of a numbered list.
  2. Visualize your data using arrays and any other programming concepts you have learned in this class. How does your visualization tells a story of your data?
      3. On your portfolio page, please include the following:
    • A description of your Data Portrait. What was the personal habit you were trying to track?
    • An instruction for the viewers on how to read your data portrait.
    • Describe your design process. Include your numbered list and any other sketches or images that would help us better understand your process.
      Your overall reflection on the assignment:
    • What did you learn about yourself through the process of self-tracking?
    • What was gained / lost through the process of data visualization?

Submission Guidelines

Please submit your sketch to two places:

  1. Submit the sketch to Canvas Assignment 6
  2. Add the sketch to your Coded Poetry portfolio
