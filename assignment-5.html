Cut-Up Poetry

Prompt

Cut-up is a technique developed by Fluxus to generatic poetry. Using YouTube or downloaded videos as your primary resource, you will collect video subtitles and collage a video or audio poem.

Coding Requirements

Use videogrep and ffmpeg to create your poem.

Submission Guidelines

Please submit an audio (mp3) or video (mp4) file to Canvas.
